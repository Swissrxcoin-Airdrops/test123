This application will be able to:
  • Access your Twitter timeline, including protected tweets, as well as your lists and collections.
  • View your Twitter profile information and account settings.
  • Review accounts you follow, as well as those you have muted or blocked.
  • Manage follow and unfollow actions on your behalf.
  • Update your profile and account settings.
  • Post and delete tweets on your behalf, and interact with tweets from others, including actions such as liking, unliking, replying, retweeting, and other forms of engagement.
  • Create, manage, and delete lists and collections on your behalf.
  • Mute, block, and report accounts on your behalf.
  • See your email address.
